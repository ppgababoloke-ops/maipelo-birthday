7 February 2000

The day the world became a little kinder

My love

You find comfort in novels, care deeply for animals, and hold your family close in a way that feels rare.

You read to escape, sleep to recharge, and cook or bake when you want to show love quietly.

These are not small things. They are the details that make you who you are.

The first time I saw you

I thought you were the most beautiful girl I had ever seen. Not dramatically — but in the way that makes your thoughts stop.

I remember my confidence disappearing, knowing that nothing I said could measure up to how I felt.

A note for you

Today isn’t about doing something big. It’s about recognising the person you are and the calm, steady light you bring into my life.

My life has been a better place because you are in it
